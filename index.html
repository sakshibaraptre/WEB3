What is the Web3 Innovation Competition?

The Web3 Innovation Competition is a global showcase for the most innovative, impactful, and inspiring Web3 projects. It is an opportunity to demonstrate your creativity, technical skills, and vision for the future of the internet.

Who Should Participate?

Developers, designers, product managers, artists, entrepreneurs, and anyone who wants to build a better internet.

Why Participate?

Showcase your work to an audience of millions, gain recognition from industry leaders, and compete for a share of $2M USD in prizes.

Submission guidelines

Your submission should include a written description of your project, along with any relevant media, code, or other materials that help explain your vision. In addition to the written description, you may also choose to submit a short video (up to 3 minutes) that demonstrates your project in action.

Submissions must be your original work. You may not submit any content that you did not create.

Submissions must be related to the theme of the competition: Web3 innovation.

Submissions must be submitted by the submission deadline: September 30, 2022.

Submissions must comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

How to submit

To submit your project to the Web3 Innovation Competition, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Write a description of your project. Your description should be between 500 and 1,000 words and should include the following information:

A brief summary of your project, A detailed explanation of how your project works, including any relevant technical details, An explanation of what makes your project innovative, impactful, or inspiring, A description of any challenges you faced during the development process and how you overcame them, Any additional information that you think is important for the judges to know

Step 2: Gather any relevant media, code, or other materials that help explain your project. This may include:

Images, diagrams, or other visual aids, Code snippets, links to code repositories, or other technical documentation, Video demonstrations (optional, up to 3 minutes in length), Any other materials that you think are relevant to your submission

Step 3: Submit your project by the submission deadline. To submit your project, please visit the Web3 Innovation Competition website and follow the instructions on the submission page. If you have any questions about the submission process, please contact the competition organizers at submit@web3innovation.com.
